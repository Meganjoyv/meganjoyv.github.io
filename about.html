Poetry should be felt in your ears, between your fingers, on the tip of your tongue, and not just left as print between pages. The content of my poetry at once grounds you and also lifts you off into spheres of large-scale perspective; the imagery may be impressionistic and also palpably specific. The multimedia nature of my work offers a holistic experience and brings the words directly to your senses. I enjoy incorporating embroidery, typewriting, paper-making, epistolary and other nostalgic medium into the structure of each piece.
